The Binding of Isaac Speed Wiki

Characters

Isaac

Stats: 3 Hearts, 3.5 Damage, 1 Speed

Starting Items: 1x Bomb

Unlocking: unlocked by default

Magdalene

Stats: 4 Hearts, 3.5 Damage, 1.3 Speed

Starting Items: Yum Heart (heals 1 full heart when used)

Unlocking: obtaining 7 or more red heart containers in one run

Cain (Generally prefered)

Stats: 2 Hearts, 3.5 Damage, 1.3 Speed

Starting Items: 1x Key, Lucky Foot (improves your luck)

Unlocking: obtaining 55 or more coins in one run

Eve

Stats: 4 Hearts, 3.5 Damage, 1.3 Speed

Starting Items: Yum Heart (heals 1 full heart when used)

Unlocking: making at least 2 deals with the devil in one run

Judas

Stats: 4 Hearts, 3.5 Damage, 1.3 Speed

Starting Items: 3x Coins, Book of Belial (increase damage and guarantees devil room)

Unlocking: defeating Satan

Items

The Fool

Effect: teleports the user to the starting room. Can be used for escaping hard rooms or ease of travel

The High Priestess

Effect: summons mom's foot to stomp enemies. Deals massive amounts of damage. Warning: if no enemies are present or you are facing mom the foot will stomp you!

The Empress

Effect: buffs the player by turning them into a demon and raising damage by 2 and speed by 1. Effects wear off when leaving the room.

The Emperor

Effect: teleports the user to the boss room. If used while fighting the boss the boss will reset!

The Lovers

Effect: summons 2 full hears.

The Chariot

Effect: similar to the unicorn. Grants you invincibility and will harm anything you touch but disables your tears until the effect wears off.

Justice

Effect: Spawns random loot like bombs, keys, and hearts.

The Hermit

Effect: teleports the user to the shop without consuming a key. If there is no shop or the player is already in it it will teleport you randomly.

Wheel of Fortune

Effect: spawns a slot machine.

Strength

Effect: increase the players size, damage, and health until they leave the room

The Hanged Man

Effect: grants the player the ability to fly till the player leaves the room

Death

Effect: deals massive amounts of damage to all enemies in the room

The Tower

Effect: spawns six random exploding bombs in the room.

The Devil

Effect: similar to the Book of Belial. Boosts damage and increases chances of encountering the devil room.

Temperace

Effect: spawns a blood donation machine.

The Stars

Effect: teleports you to the treasure room without consuming a key.

The Moon

Effect: teleports the player to the secret room. One random wall will be opened to allow the player to exit without the usage of a bomb

The Sun

Effect: completely heals the player, damages all enemies, reveals the entire map, and removes the effects of the Curse of Darkness

Judgement

Effect: spawns the poor homeless dude (beggar). TIP: using this in a devil room or angel room will mean the beggar grants you items specific to the room!

The World

Effect: reveals the entire map and clears effects from the Curse of the Darkness.
